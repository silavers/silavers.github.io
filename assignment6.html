Assignment 6: Molding & Casting

3d print of the Molds-for-Molds

I started by creating this boolean merge of 3 shapes to create the Harry Potter Deathly Hallows sign.

I did a draft angle analysis, used boolean difference & merge to create keys and sprues. I added the sprue to the flat side and decided to make it lay upside down so the triangle points weren't affected.

Time was definitely not on my side due to some time sucking print fails. In order to speed up my final print, I lowered the quality, made it smaller, increased the speed and lowered the infill amount.
