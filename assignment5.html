Assignment 5: Lamps part 2! And molding and casting part 1!

Final Lamp

After lots (and I mean lots) of pivots & misprints, my vision finally came to fruition.

Added a base

I originally thought the candle would look good freestanding, which is why I incorporated a cutout for the chord. But it didn't feel quite right and I wanted to make it more stable.

So, I decided to add a base.

The base was designed so that the candle could wedge into the hole and the lamp "innards" could fit, with a chord "channel" of sorts, so it could easily lay down and exit the lamp. It took 3 prints to get the hole in the middle to be the perfect "wedge" size.


STL Files & Printing Notes

A few tricks that really helped me was lowering the fan speed so that it didnt cool as quickly, and I used a zigzag infill design so the light didnt look as "square"-y,
