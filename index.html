Sil takes on Digital Fabrication!

👋 I'm Sil.
I'm a Product Manager by day and a 2nd year HCDE Masters Student on Wednesday nights.

I approach my work with these mantras:
- It takes a village to build quality products
- Never stop asking "why?"
- No detail is too small to improve

In my free time, I enjoy snowboarding 🏂, mountain biking 🚵‍, and taking photos 📸.

I've always been a tinkerer of sorts and have made my own coasters, jewelry, and I always make snowglobes around the holidays. My interest in digital fabrication is to make my arts & crafts tendency a little more useful.

I know enough HTML & CSS from my old Myspace days and enough Photoshop & Illustrator to get by in 508. I have experience as a UX Researcher as well as product management, so I am happy to teach what I know in those departments if the opportunity arises! But mostly excited to learn from you all as Digital Fabrication is a whole new world for me.
