Sil takes on Digital Fabrication!

I'm Sil. I'm a Product Manager by day and an 2nd year HCDE Masters Student on Wednesday nights. I approach my work with these mantras: - It takes a village to build quality products - Listen with curiosity and never stop asking "why?" - Keep the big picture in mind, but no detail is too small to improve

In my free time, I enjoy snowboarding, mountain biking, and taking photos. I've always been a tinkerer of sorts and have made my own coasters, jewelry, and I always make snowglobes around the holidays. My interest in digital fabrication is to make my arts & crafts tendency a little more useful.
