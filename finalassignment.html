Final Project: Concrete & Metal Jewelry Collection


Concept

For my final project, I made a mini jewelry collection. Inspired by the mix of concrete and metal jewelry trend, and seeing that proto pasta sold metal filament, I thought it would be interesting to see if I could mix the 3d printing with casting.

Jewelry Line

Concept & Inspo

Inspiration

The concrete jewlery inspo below all are either painted or include metals. My goal is to include 3s printed metals.

I'm excited about this project because it brings a few challenges including creating a string hole in the pendants and crucial attention to size detail in order to ensure the mold and the 3d printed pieces that will go in it fit perfectly.
